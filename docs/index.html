StuHub Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 4, 2026

Last Updated: July 4, 2026

Welcome to StuHub. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the StuHub mobile application.

StuHub is an offline-first application. Most of your academic data is stored locally on your device. Cloud backup is optional and only happens when you choose to use the Backup feature.

1. Information We Collect

  • Username
  • Email Address
  • Password (stored securely in hashed form)

2. Information Stored on Your Device

To support offline functionality, StuHub stores the following locally:

  • Username
  • Email Address
  • Profile Photo (if selected)
  • Academic Session
  • Subjects
  • Timetable
  • Attendance Records
  • Attendance Analytics
  • App Preferences

3. Cloud Backup

Cloud backup is completely optional and only occurs when you manually tap the Backup button.

The following data may be backed up:

  • Username
  • Email Address
  • Academic Session
  • Subjects
  • Timetable
  • Attendance Records

The following data is not uploaded:

  • Profile Photo
  • Local App Preferences

4. Restore

When you choose Restore, only information previously backed up to the cloud is restored. Items stored only on your device, such as your profile photo, are not restored.

5. Password Reset Emails

StuHub uses Brevo solely to send password reset emails. Your email address is not used for advertising or marketing communications.

6. Authentication & Security

  • HTTPS encrypted communication
  • JWT Authentication
  • Secure password hashing

7. Third-Party Services

  • AWS EC2 – Backend Hosting
  • MongoDB Atlas – Cloud Database
  • Brevo – Password Reset Email Delivery

8. Your Choices

  • You may update your account information.
  • You may log out at any time.
  • Logging out removes locally stored application data from your device.
  • Previously backed up cloud data remains associated with your account and can be restored after signing in again.
  • You can choose whether or not to use Cloud Backup.

9. Children's Privacy

StuHub is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the Effective Date.
