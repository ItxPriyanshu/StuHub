StuHub Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your StuHub account and associated cloud data, please send an email from your registered email address to:

Email:
priyanshukumardev01@gmail.com

Subject:
Account Deletion Request

Information to include

  • Your registered email address
  • Your username (optional)

What will be deleted

  • Your StuHub account
  • Cloud backup data
  • Attendance records stored on our servers
  • Subjects
  • Timetable
  • Academic session data

What will NOT be deleted automatically

  • Data stored locally on your device. You can remove it by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days.
